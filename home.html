Cavite State University - Bacoor Campus

Dedicated to providing quality education and shaping future leaders, Cavite State University (CVSU) Bacoor Campus is where students from diverse backgrounds come together to pursue their dreams.

CVSU offers a wide range of programs and courses, fostering academic excellence and innovation. Our faculty and staff are committed to nurturing the talents of our students and preparing them for a successful future.

At CVSU Bacoor Campus, we take pride in our state-of-the-art facilities, a vibrant community, and a supportive learning environment. We believe that learning today is the foundation for leading tomorrow.
